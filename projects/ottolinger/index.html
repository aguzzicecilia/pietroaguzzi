Ottolinger is a brand founded by Cosima Gradient and Christa Bösch. Fabrics are manipulated and deconstructed, torn, sewn together with crude stitching or even fused together. A circuit of construction, deconstruction and reconstruction. Ottolinger's style focuses on beauty, not beauty in the classical sense, but that which leaves room for strangeness. With Giga, Art direction Kasper-Florio.
