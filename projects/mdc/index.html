MASSIMODECARLO gallery was founded in 1987, the Gallery has played a fundamental role in the contemporary art scene by promoting a vital dialogue between artists and national and international. MASSIMODECARLO works with an extraordinary variety of artists across mediums, from painting, drawing, installation, sculpture, photography, and performance to video. Over the years, the gallery’s artists have gained international recognition, showing their work in galleries, museums and biennials across the world and by accessing outstanding public and private collections. With Giga.
