FabricLive is a compilation mix of the London club Fabric, established in November 2001 and ended in November 2018. Emerging and established DJs publish monthly a selection of songs. In this rebranding proposal, each vinyl has as its cover a general view of all the tracks included in the mix, arranged according to their duration and the value of the decibels. On the back of the vinyl you can find the songs contained in the compilation displayed on the basis of three values: bpm, energy and danceability. With Calibro.
