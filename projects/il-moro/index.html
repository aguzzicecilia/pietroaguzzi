Brand Identity of a craft brewery in Vigevano, through which a visual based on the medieval origins of the city was created (the name of the brewery, in fact, derives from the street in which it is located, or “Via Ludovico il Moro”). The typographic approach is therefore linked to the Gothic, while the more innovative side of the brand is represented by the labels of different colors of the various bottles.
