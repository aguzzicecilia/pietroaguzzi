For the 2024 edition, we collaborated with Valentina Cameranesi Sgroi and the Loewe Foundation to create a contrast between the visual clarity of Charles Negre's cut-out photographs and the raw aesthetic of the artists' process, captured in amateur phone videos. The homepage showcases the floating artworks, while each artist's page takes the user on a journey from the final piece back to the artist's studio, featuring materials, tools, and sounds through onomatopoeia. With Giga.
