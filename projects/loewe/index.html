For the 2024 edition, we collaborated with Valentina Cameranesi Sgroi and the Loewe Foundation team to explore the contrast between the clean visuals of artwork cut-outs and the raw aesthetic of the artists’ process. The cut-outs, photographed by Charles Negre, are paired with amateur phone-shot videos documenting the creative journey. The homepage features a carousel of floating artwork cut-outs, while artist pages narrate a reverse journey—from the finished piece back to the studio, showcasing materials, tools, and work sounds through onomatopoeia. With Giga.
